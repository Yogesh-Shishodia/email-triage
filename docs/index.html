How it works

Three quiet steps. Hours back every day.

InboxIQ runs in the background, classifying every incoming email and preparing replies for the ones that need them. Your team reviews — they don't react.

01

Connect

Securely connect Gmail, Outlook, or any IMAP mailbox in under two minutes. Single sign-on, role-based access, and audit logs come standard.

02

Triage

Our AI classifies each message into the categories that matter to your team, scores urgency, extracts deadlines and key entities, and drafts a reply when one is warranted.

03

Approve

Review the day's prepared replies on one screen. Edit if needed, approve, and send — every message threaded into the original conversation correctly.

Common questions

Things teams ask before they sign.

How quickly will my team see results?

Most teams report measurable time savings within the first week. Voice-and-tone calibration takes about 14 days to reach its best quality; after that, drafts are usable as-is for roughly 75% of common email types. Given that knowledge workers reclaim ~11 hours a week of email time at full productivity (McKinsey baseline), payback typically lands within the first month.

Which email providers do you support?

Gmail (Workspace and personal), Microsoft 365 (Outlook), and any IMAP-compatible mailbox. We also support shared inboxes (e.g. support@, billing@) with assignment routing.

Can we deploy this on our own infrastructure?

Yes. We offer cloud, hybrid (your data perimeter, our intelligence layer), and full on-premise deployments. Enterprise customers can also bring their own model — we support OpenAI, Anthropic, Azure OpenAI, AWS Bedrock, and self-hosted models like Llama and Qwen.

Will the AI ever send an email without my approval?

Never, by default. Every draft requires a human approval before send — that's the central safety guarantee. Enterprise plans can configure auto-send rules for narrow categories (e.g. "send acknowledgments automatically for invoices under $500") with full audit trails.

How do we customize the categories for our team?

Start with one of our defaults (Sales, Customer Support, Recruiting, Finance, Executive) and modify, or build a taxonomy from scratch with your team. Categories define how the AI classifies, drafts, and prioritizes — anything expressible as a prompt is configurable per team. A typical custom configuration takes one 60-minute working session with our team.

What about training and onboarding?

Onboarding is included on all plans. Implementation includes a setup call, custom prompt tuning for your categories, voice calibration on a sample of your sent folder, and a 30-day success milestone review.
